About IToolbox

Building tomorrow's solutions today

Who We Are

IToolbox is a technology startup founded in 2019, focused on developing innovative digital solutions that leverage AI, IoT, and modern web technologies. We're on a mission to create practical, scalable products that solve real-world problems across both public and private sectors.

Our Leadership

Led by Nicole John A. Mortel (Founding President & CEO), a Sr. Network Engineer with expertise in full-stack development, AI/ML, and network infrastructure. Currently in our bootstrap phase, our CEO leads all technical and business functions while building our foundation products.

Growth Plans

We're strategically expanding our team with planned hires in 2026, including a CTO/Lead Developer and Marketing & Sales Lead. As we scale, we'll grow into a full executive team with specialized roles in technology, operations, finance, and customer success.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, user-friendly digital products that combine cutting-edge technology with practical applications. We serve all industries requiring urgent technological innovation to drive efficiency and growth—from environmental initiatives to educational platforms, including Agriculture, Healthcare, Financial Services (Fintech), Logistics, and Manufacturing.

Our Vision

IToolbox embraces emerging technology trends and inspires professionals to harness technology as a service—driving innovation while caring for the environment and uplifting society. As the leading provider of IT resources, IToolbox will guide and empower fellow professionals with knowledge, tools, and support. Beyond technology, we are committed to contributing to society by extending opportunities and resources to those in need, ensuring that progress benefits not only businesses but also communities.
